Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a very simple dish which anybody can cook.

Despite requiring little effort, it's really delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1L milk
  • 450g macaroni
  • 200g shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring the milk to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add the pasta and stir constantly for 10 minutes or until the pasta is cooked.
  3. Turn off the heat and add the cheese.
  4. Stir until the cheese is melted.
