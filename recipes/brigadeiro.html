Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a very common Brazilian dessert made with chocolate.

It is very popular amongst people of all ages and its most usual consumption is during birthday parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 400g sweetened condensed milk
  • 30g cocoa powder
  • 150g chocolate sprinkle

Steps

  1. Over low heat, melt the butter, condensed milk, and cocoa powder in a pot, stirring consistently.
  2. Stir until you can see the bottom of the pot for 2-3 seconds when dragging a spon through.
  3. Pour the mix onto a greased plate and let it chill for one hour.
  4. Shape and roll the mix into balls.
  5. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles.
